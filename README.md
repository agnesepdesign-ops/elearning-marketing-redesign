# Marketing Area Redesign - Aladia's e-learning platform
UI/UX case study: redesign of the marketing section of an e-learning platform, from research to prototyping in Figma.

The goal was to find conversion points on the landing page and improve the user experience, demonstrating skills in **analysis, wireframing, UI design, and prototyping** applied to a real digital product context.

## üß† Method & Process
The project has been developed following a structured process: 

**1. Analysis**
- Analysis of the content structure e information hierarchy
- Evaluation call-to-action and navigation flow
- Main UX constraints identification

**1.2 Competitor Analysis**
- Udemy is one of the main player in e-learning segment, intersting to undersatnd their UI and marketing touch points flow. Focus on generating high volume, income, and visibility for those who publish courses.
- Teachable is focused more on content creation and conversion, the goal is to offer the best "self-school creation experience".
- Zoom one of the main player in "live and collanborating meetings".
- Moodle is useful to better understand how the LMS (leasrning management system) works using a widely used platform as reference. 

**2. Wireframing**
- Creation of low-fidelity wireframes
- Redefinition of the structure/flow of marketing pages

**3. UI Design**
The visual hierarchy has been improved through targeted layout and spacing choices.

- The introduction of a **predominantly white background** has increased negative space, lightening the interface and improving content readability.
- The more structured use of **spacing between sections** helps users clearly distinguish between different blocks of information.
- **More calls to action have been added within each section**, with the aim of guiding users as they navigate and making the path to action more intuitive and action-oriented.

- Strategic use of typography and color, redesigning primary, secondary and tertiary buttons folowing Aladia brand colors. 
- Designing reusable components.

**4. Prototyping**
- Interactive prototype to simulate the user experience
- Presentation of the complete workflow of the marketing section
